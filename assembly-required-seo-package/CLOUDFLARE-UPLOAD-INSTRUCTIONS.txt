Cloudflare Pages Upload Instructions

1. Unzip assembly-required-cloudflare-ready.zip.
2. Open the unzipped folder.
3. Drag the CONTENTS of this folder into Cloudflare Pages Upload Assets.

The files you drag should include:
- index.html
- about.html
- services.html
- contact.html
- images folder
- sitemap.xml
- robots.txt

Important:
Cloudflare needs index.html at the top level.

After upload:
Open the temporary pages.dev link and test the site.

Before final domain launch:
Replace all REPLACE-WITH-MARKATE-LINK buttons with your actual Markate booking link.
